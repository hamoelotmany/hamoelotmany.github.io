  • Welcome to my website!

    I am currently an Assistant Lecturer at TSE. I received my Master's degree in Applied Financial Mathematics from the University of Marne-la-Vallée and École des Ponts in 2012. In November 2015, I earned my PhD in Applied Mathematics under the supervision of Daniela Capatina (MCF-HDR) and Didier Graebling (Prof..) at the University of Pau, Pau, France. From 2015 to 2017, I worked as a Postdoctoral Scholar under the supervision of Dr. Reda Baina at the OPERA (Applied Research Petroleum Organization) In Pau. From 2017 to 2019, I continued my postdoctoral research under the supervision of Prof. Philippe Poncet (LMAP, University of Pau) and Dr. Tatiana Chugunova (CSTJF, Total). I am currently In charge of collaborative research projects with

    • Mhamed Eddahbi ( meddahbi@ksu.edu.sa) : stochastic partial differential equations, simulation & optimization
    • Pascal Begout ( Pascal.Begout@math.cnrs.fr) : Numerical analysis for the damped Schrödinger equation with a sublinear nonlinearity in an unbounded domain

    Research Interests:
    • Numerical analysis, Finite element approximation, NXFEM methods.
    • Inverse problem with deterministic and stochastic methods, Neural network Inversion.
    • Stochastic modelling and simulaion for PDEs
    • Numerical analysis of stochastic partial differential equations
    Applications:
    • Energy system and dispersed media (calorific capacity, Phase Change Materials)
    • Fluid mechanics and rheology (FluidFlow, Navier-Stokes, Giesekus model, modelling of red blood flows)

    This is my professional website, which I use to record and share my research, teaching interests and experiences. Do not hesitate to contact me with research and collaboration ideas. I look forward to hearing from you.
